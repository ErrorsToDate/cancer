Home

Cancer today affects millions of people as a inhibition in their daily lives. As high schoolers, making special care-packages for local patients is our way to help those fighting cancer. Together, we collect and donate items to the NCCC who distributes them to their patients as our way of saying that we both care and want to help. If you are interested in supporting our cause, please head here to learn how to help or how to contact us
